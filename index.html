Plate Tectonics is a theory that explains why we have continents and the various features of the earth’s oceans. The world is divided into 9 major plates that move over the earth’s inner layer. When these plates collide they create mountain ridges and valleys even on the ocean’s floor. Some plates move up to 4 inches per year (sounds small but adds up given millions of years). Author Becky Oskin explains the history attributed to that theory stating : While the Earth is 4.54 billion years old, because oceanic crust is constantly recycled at subduction zones, he oldest seafloor is only about 200 million years old). As a kid, I was very interested in geography,geology and world history and I probably knew the capital of every country in the world. (I definitely can't do that anymore) This project is what I would have loved to have when I was 6. This is a dynamic webpage connected to a MySQL database and using a serverless function to transfer data through a REST API . Each continent includes capital, official languages, time zones and population data among other things. This website also includes dynamically generated quizzes on each page to test your knowledge. A case insensitive searchbar allows you to search any content on the page. My hope is that this is as instructive as it is entertaining.
